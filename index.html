What is TBCC CLOUD™?
TBCC CLOUD™ stands for 'Open-Source Digital Assets Exchange'. It is a hybrid open-source software consisting of public and private libraries, designed to build a fully-featured exchange service to facilitate the trading of digital assets, cryptocurrencies, and security tokens. The mission of TBCC CLOUD™ is to enable you to operate a robust financial SaaS marketplace with native high security and risk control.
